Common Questions

How to request blood?

Go to the "Request Blood" section, fill in the patient details and post your request. It will be visible to all donors.

Is this service free?

Yes, Helix is a non-profit platform created to connect donors and patients for free.

How can I become a donor?

Simply register an account, and your information will be listed in the "Find Donor" section.
